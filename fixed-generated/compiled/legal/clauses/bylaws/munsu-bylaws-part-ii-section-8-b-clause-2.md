# DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES 2

## Compiled Truth

Generated aid: this page represents one parsed legal unit. Exact source text remains authoritative.

## Source Text

2. Faculty/School Directors shall:
a. be students within the ward they represent;
b. serve on the Faculty or Academic Council of their ward, wherever student representative positions exist for which they are eligible;
c. provide assistance with the campaigns, initiatives, and services of the Union, to a minimum of three (3) hours per week outside of meetings of the Board and the Standing Committees of Union. At least one (1) of those hours must be scheduled office hours; alternate arrangements may be made in the case of extenuating circumstances.
