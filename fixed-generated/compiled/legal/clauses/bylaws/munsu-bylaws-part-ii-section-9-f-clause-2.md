## Source Text

2. If a member of the Board of Directors must be absent from a regular meeting of the Board of Directors, meetings of the Standing Committees of the Union, or meetings deemed necessary for the execution of a member’s duties of office, they are expected to send their regrets to the Secretary to the Board no less than twenty-four (24) hours prior to the scheduled meeting time.
a. Regrets communicated to the Secretary to the Board with less than twenty-four (24) hours notice shall be considered an absence for the purpose of automatic reprimand per §14.D
