## Source Text

2. Upon missing an additional one (1) regular Board meeting, meeting of the Standing Committees of the Union, or meeting deemed necessary for the execution of a member’s duties of office after automatic reprimand, without regrets, the member will be automatically censured and notice of the censure will be served at the next regular meeting of the Board.
