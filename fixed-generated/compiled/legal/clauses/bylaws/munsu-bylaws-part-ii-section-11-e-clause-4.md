## Source Text

4. Non- Academic Recognition or Ratification will be granted to the following:
a. Groups not based on a formal academic discipline.
b. Any group formed around a common interest or activity that can be shown to contribute to the university community and, in particular, student life.
c. Any club and society that for any reason operates without a constitution shall be governed by the MUNSU Constitution and Bylaws and the MUNSU Sample constitution for Clubs and Societies.
