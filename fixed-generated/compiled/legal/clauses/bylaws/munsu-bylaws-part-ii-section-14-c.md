## Source Text

C. RECALL
a. Faculty representatives may be recalled by a petition of one third (1/3) of their faculty.
b. Directors at Large may be recalled by a petition of one third (1/3) of the Union membership.
c. Constituency representatives may be recalled by a petition of one third (1/3) of the Union membership.
d. Within one (1) week of the filing of the petition of recall, the Board shall call a by-election to fill the vacant position at the earliest possible date.
e. A Board member who has been recalled may re-contest the seat.
f. Any petition for recall shall be fixed with the date for commencement and shall be invalid if not filed with the Board within three (3) weeks. The petition may be filed with the Secretary to the Board of Directors.
g. An Executive Director may be recalled by a petition and referendum. A petition of one third (1/3) of the membership shall be presented calling for the recall of the Executive Director. A referendum shall then be held as soon as possible, within regulations for a referenda, to decide the issue.
h. The members of the Board and Executive Directors of MUNSU can only be recalled individually.
