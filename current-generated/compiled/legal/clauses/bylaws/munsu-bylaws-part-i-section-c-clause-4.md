# INCORPORATION 4

## Compiled Truth

Generated aid: this page represents one parsed legal unit. Exact source text remains authoritative.

## Source Text

4. The property and assets of that unincorporated body known as the Memorial University of Newfoundland Students’ Union shall as from the date of the coming into force of this Act become the property of the Union and shall be vested in it, and all liabilities of that body as of such date shall become liabilities of the Union and shall be paid and satisfied by it.
