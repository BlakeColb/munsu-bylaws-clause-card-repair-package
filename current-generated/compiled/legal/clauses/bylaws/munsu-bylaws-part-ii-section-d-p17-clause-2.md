## Source Text

2. The appointment of the representative is to be overseen by submission of a nominations package for the position in question with the requisite number of signatures to the MUNSU front desk email, and three out of the five executive directors, and subsequently to be approved by a simple majority vote at the next meeting of the Board of Directors. a. In the case of multiple submissions, the first received submission shall be voted on first. Should all vacant positions be filled by vote, no other submissions shall be considered for that position. b. If a nomination package is rejected during the Board of Directors vote, the candidate in question cannot run until the next By-Election or General Election. MUNSU website or else on request. must be time stamped and signed by two witnesses upon receipt at the MUNSU office.
