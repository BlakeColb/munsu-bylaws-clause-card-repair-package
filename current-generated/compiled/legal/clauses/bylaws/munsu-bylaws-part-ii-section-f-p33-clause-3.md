## Source Text

3. A member of the Board of Directors who is unable to attend a regular meeting of the same may indicate to the Secretary to the Board that another member of the Board shall serve as their proxy for the duration of the meeting no less than twenty-four (24) hours prior to the scheduled meeting time. a. The individual identified as a proxy must provide written agreement to the Secretary to the Board b. Only elected members of the Board may serve as proxy voters proxy vote for the duration of a single meeting. e. Proxies may only be identified for regular meetings of the Board of Directors, and not other meetings of the Union.
